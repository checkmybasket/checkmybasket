# Rebrand Brief: GiftCircle → CheckMyBasket

**Domain:** checkmybasket.co.uk (existing — currently a comparison site, to be replaced)
**Change:** Full rebrand of the Secret Santa group gifting app from "GiftCircle" to "CheckMyBasket"
**Website replacement instructions:** To follow separately

---

## 1. Why This Name Works

"CheckMyBasket" was originally a comparison site, but the name naturally pivots to group gifting:

- **"Check"** — check your match, check your wishlist, check who's joined, check your predictions
- **"My Basket"** — your gift basket, what's in your Secret Santa haul, your group's gift collection
- **"Check my basket"** as a phrase — the natural thing you say when browsing gifts: "let me check my basket"

The name has a warm, shopping-friendly tone that fits the gifting context without being locked to Christmas. It works for Secret Santa, birthday wishlists, Eid exchanges, and year-round gifting — just as "GiftCircle" did, but with stronger UK commercial energy and an existing domain with history.

### SEO Advantage

The domain checkmybasket.co.uk already exists and has history. Any existing domain authority, backlinks, and Google indexing carry over. This is a head start over registering a new domain from scratch. The .co.uk TLD reinforces UK-first positioning.

The homepage can still target the primary keyword with:

> **Free Secret Santa Generator with Wishlists and Gift Ideas**

The brand name doesn't need to contain "Secret Santa" — it just needs to not conflict with it.

## 3. Brand Identity Updates

### Logo Direction

The CheckMyBasket logo should work at three scales:
- **Full logo:** "CheckMyBasket" wordmark with an icon
- **Short logo:** "CMB" monogram or icon only (for app icons, favicons, small spaces)
- **Favicon:** Icon only, recognisable at 32×32px

**Icon concept options:**

| Concept | Description | Why |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| Gift basket | A simple basket silhouette with a gift bow or ribbon | Direct connection to "basket" + gifting |
| Tick/check + basket | A checkmark integrated into a basket shape | Plays on "check" in the name |
| Shopping basket with a star/sparkle | A basket with a festive sparkle element | Warm, celebratory, gift-forward |
| Basket with a question mark | Nods to the mystery element of Secret Santa | Playful, curiosity-driven |

**Recommendation:** A gift basket with a subtle checkmark integrated into the handle or ribbon. Clean, modern, works at small sizes. Not overly festive — should work year-round.

### Colour Palette

No change to the colour system. The tokens defined in the Claude Code brief remain:

- Primary: Deep forest green (#1B4332)
- Accent: Rich berry red (#C1121F)
- Warm: Gold (#D4A574)
- Background: Warm cream (#FFF8F0)
- Surface: White (#FFFFFF)

These colours are season-neutral and work with the CheckMyBasket name. The green/cream/red palette doesn't need to change with the rebrand.

### Typography

No change. Fraunces for headings, DM Sans for body text. The typography system is brand-independent.

### Tone of Voice

No change to the tone guidelines. Warm, confident, slightly cheeky, British. The CheckMyBasket name actually suits a slightly more playful tone than GiftCircle — "basket" has a down-to-earth, everyday quality that encourages casual language.

**Example tone shift:**
- GiftCircle: "Create your gift exchange"
- CheckMyBasket: "Sort your Secret Santa"

The CheckMyBasket name lends itself to more direct, colloquial British English. Lean into that.

## 5. Domain and Infrastructure

### Domain Setup

- **Primary domain:** `www.checkmybasket.co.uk`
- **Redirect:** `checkmybasket.co.uk` → `www.checkmybasket.co.uk`
- **Invite links:** `www.checkmybasket.co.uk/join/[code]`
- **Gift pages:** `www.checkmybasket.co.uk/gifts/under-10`

### Old Site Handling

The existing checkmybasket.co.uk content (supermarket/skincare comparison) will be replaced entirely. Instructions for the transition will follow separately. Key considerations:

- **301 redirects:** If any existing pages have SEO value or inbound links, set up 301 redirects to relevant new pages (e.g., old homepage → new homepage). This preserves any domain authority.
- **Sitemap update:** Generate and submit a new sitemap to Google Search Console after the switchover.
- **Google Search Console:** Verify ownership, submit new sitemap, request indexing of key pages.
- **Existing backlinks:** Audit what links point to the old site. If any have value, preserve them with redirects.

### Environment Variable Update

```
NEXT_PUBLIC_APP_URL=https://www.checkmybasket.co.uk
```

All other environment variables (Supabase, Resend, etc.) remain unchanged.

The rebrand is a name change, not a product change. The following remain identical:

- Typography system
